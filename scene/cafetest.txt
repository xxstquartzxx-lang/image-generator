### Ultimate Photography Prompt for Minimalist Lifestyle Fashion

**Concept:**
A refined, minimalist lifestyle fashion scene featuring a natural Japanese woman, standing calmly and elegantly near a window in a minimal cafe, embodying refined everyday beauty with negative space.

**Subject Details:**
A natural Japanese woman, late 20s to early 30s, realistic beauty, with minimal makeup and natural hair. A calm and elegant expression. She is the centered subject. The framing should be a full-body or mid-length shot.

**Exact Outfit Description (MATCH ALL DETAILS):**
An olive khaki (specifically, matte, earthy olive) wide-leg camisole jumpsuit with delicate, thin spaghetti straps, layered over a deep black (or dark charcoal) sleeveless crew-neck top. The outfit's specific design, flowing wide legs, fabric textures (realistic crepe for the jumpsuit, fine knit for the top), and the layered fit must be an *exact match* to the provided style reference images. Focus on the realistic fabric weight and drape.

**Setting & Environment:**
Standing in a minimalist Japanese cafe, near a large window. The atmosphere is calm and elegant. Abundant soft, natural daylight filters through the window, highlighting realistic textures. The environment is a neutral beige (oatmeal, warm concrete) with textured walls and light wood elements, featuring generous negative space to emphasize the minimal aesthetic.

**Lighting & Texture:**
Soft, diffused daylight (no harsh shadows), creating realistic textures on the fabric, skin, and environment. A high-quality filmic texture with natural grain. Realistic depth of field (shallow), with the subject in sharp focus.

**Composition:**
Vertical composition with a 4:5 aspect ratio. The subject is centered. Generous negative space around the subject. A stable, composed stance.

**CRITICAL CONSTRAINT:**
NO TEXT, NO LOGOS, NO BRANDING on any part of the image, the outfit, or the environment. Absolutely clean image.
